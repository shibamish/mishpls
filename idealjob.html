The Ideal Job!

Before diving into some job listings that appeal to me, let's quickly go over what I want from a job! For me, a job is almost like your second life and your co-workers soon become a second family, so it's super important to find a role that suits your personality and needs.

My personal job checklist:

  • Accessible location, close to a train station or close to home. Or maybe even potential to move!
  • Good incentives, goal-orientated and KPI's. And by KPI, I mean team/personal KPI's as opposed to when co-workers compete to stay at the top.
  • Work benefits, whether that be health wise or environment wise, knowing the company cares about their employees means the employees will care about the company!
  • Work/life balance
  • Potential for career growth because I want to grow with the company too!
  • Variety! Lots and lots of change, projects and growth!

Therefore, despite this project and the experience and rewards it comes with, the role itself seems a bit too unabalanced.

Job Listing Rating: 9/10

With that in mind, in order for me to even get the job after graduating, I would need to start building up experience within the gaming development field. I may also potentially need to transfer over to B CompSci (hopefully!) or maybe to something more game-based.
